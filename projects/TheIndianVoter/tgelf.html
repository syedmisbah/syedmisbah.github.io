tGELF

The Global Education & Leadership Foundation (tGELF) is a program initiative of The Nand & Jeet Khemka Foundation whose vision is to nurture young ethical leaders that will be future agents of change. tGELF has a unique value based curriculum, state-of-the-art training and world class assessments which it implements across 75 schools pan India, 500,000 students and now in 6 countries.
