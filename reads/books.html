How do I read a specific book on my kindle?

  1. Find the book you want to read by searching for it. (Hint: Use Ctrl+F)
  2. Note down the author's name
  3. Go to this link.
  4. Find your author and open his/her folder
  5. Download the corresponding .MOBI file to your PC/mobile.
  6. Find your Kindle's send to email address. Explained here.
  7. Send the MOBI file to the Kindle's email through your registered email ID.
  8. Voila! That's it. The book will appear on your kindle device in a few moments.
